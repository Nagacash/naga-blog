Woodland360's Rap Cypher is a monthly event that brings together some of the best rappers in the area to compete for prizes and bragging rights. The event is open to all ages and levels of experience, and has something for everyone. There are three rounds of competition, and each month the winner gets a cash prize and a spot in the final cypher of the year. The cypher is hosted by Woodland360, and is sponsored by local businesses..
